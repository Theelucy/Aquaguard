Privacy Policy

At Aquaguard, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when using our services.

1. Information Collection

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, and phone number when you voluntarily provide it to us. Additionally, we collect information on your usage of our website and services.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except when necessary to provide services to you. Third parties with whom we share information are contractually obligated to maintain its confidentiality.

4. Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and use industry-standard methods to protect it. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, and we encourage you to take precautions to protect your data.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. You can control how cookies are used by adjusting your browser settings.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are encouraged to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of any changes.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at support@liquidvision.com.
